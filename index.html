About me

I am working on microbiome and bioinformatics research, with interests in developing analytical and visualizing tools for multi-omics and and statistical genomics.

Research

  • Novel PERMANOVA-based approach for pathway enrichment that yields improved sensitivity and error-control
  • Identifying three risk factors for skin and soft tissue infection from patients
  • Meta-analysis of metabolic disease microbiome at inter-species and intra-species levels
  • Investigating the variations of different F.praunitzii strains' gene expression for IBD biomarkers

Leadership

  • 4K and half-marathon runner for Nationwide Children's Hospital, OhioHealth and OSU Wexner Medical Center
